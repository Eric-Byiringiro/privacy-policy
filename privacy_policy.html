📦 Information We Collect

We collect only the minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Device Information: Basic device data (model, OS version) used only to ensure the app works correctly on your device.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about which flyers and categories are viewed most, to help us improve our content.
  • Language Preference: Your selected language (Kinyarwanda, English, French, or Swahili) to show you relevant content.
  • Messages/Feedback: If you choose to send us a message through the app, we collect the content of that message only.
⚠️ We do NOT collect your name, email address, phone number, precise location, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it to us.

🔍 How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected only to:

  • Deliver spiritual, health, and family content in your preferred language
  • Improve app performance and content quality
  • Send notifications about newly uploaded flyers (only with your permission)
  • Respond to messages and feedback you send us
  • Track anonymous usage statistics to understand what content is most helpful
💡 We never sell, rent, or trade your information to any third party for commercial purposes. Your data is used solely to serve you better.

🔗 Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following trusted third-party services to function:

  • Firebase (by Google): Used for data storage, synchronization, analytics, and push notifications.   Firebase Privacy Policy →
  • Cloudinary: Used to store and deliver flyer images efficiently to your device.   Cloudinary Privacy Policy →
These services may collect anonymous usage data as described in their respective privacy policies. We do not share your personal data with any other third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the app.

🔔 Push Notifications

With your explicit permission, we may send push notifications to inform you when new flyers or content is uploaded. These notifications are purely informational and gospel-centered.

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings without affecting your ability to use the app.

💾 Offline Storage

The app allows you to download flyers to your device for offline viewing. These files are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to our servers or shared with anyone.

You can delete downloaded content at any time through the app settings or your device storage manager.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All content is focused on Christian values, health, and family matters in a safe and appropriate way.

🔒 Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. All data transmitted between the app and our servers uses secure HTTPS encryption. Firebase, our primary data platform, maintains industry-standard security certifications.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use the app responsibly.

⚖️ Your Rights

As a user of our app, you have the right to:

  • Use the app fully without providing any personal information
  • Enable or disable push notifications at any time
  • Delete all downloaded content from your device at any time
  • Request deletion of any messages or feedback you have submitted
  • Ask us what data we hold about you at any time

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our app evolves. Any changes will be reflected with a new effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
